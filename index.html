About

I've been working in IT for 3 years now, 2 of those at a local MSP, where I've gotten hands-on experience accross dozens of different environments, tools, and network setups. Through that exposure I've built a solid understanding of how systems, networks, and infrastructure function together in the real world.

Over time, I've gravitated towards every cyber security ticket and problem I could get my hands on. I've led projects that measurably improved the security posture for dozens of clients, and that work turned into a genuine passion. I'm now working on transitioning into cyber security full-time, and this page is where I document that process. By sharing my projects, learning, and growth, I hope to find like-minded people to solve real cyber security problems and share our passion for this field.
